So, this is the last page the website. On this page I would like to give you some tips for resume.

  1. Customize your resume

    Tailor your resume for each job application by highlighting the skills and experience most relevant to the specific position.

  1. Use a professional font

    Choose a standard, easily readable font like Arial or Times New Roman.

  1. Choose the right resume format

    The chronological format is a good starting point, but consider a functional or combination format if you have limited work experience or are highlighting specific skills.
